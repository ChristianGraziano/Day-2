Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetaizer topped with sweet Caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy Brie. It will become you new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teapons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teapons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,think sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oli, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. in a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togeter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorted. You don't need to actually knead the dought,thougt i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
